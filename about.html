About Us

Our confectionery was founded in 1998 and is famous for the high quality standards of its products. Unexpected flavor combinations will not leave anyone indifferent.

Here you can taste crumbly biscuits, panettone, pastries, croissants and a variety of handmade chocolates.

The confectionery also offers two new creams with dark chocolate and sugar-free hazelnuts, made according to a special formula.The outstanding quality of chocolate is primarily based on personal tastings of the best samples of cocoa beans from Venezuela, Ecuador, Colombia, Madagascar.

Our cakes and pastries with chocolate, whipped cream, custard, fruits, nuts and berries, all kinds of pastries and sweets are highly regarded by the most fastidious sweet tooth around the world.

In addition to baked goods, the confectionery presents an excellent collection of teas: Indian, Ceylon, Chinese, Himalayan, African, Georgian, Irish, Kenyan, Vietnamese, with chocolate, citrus, cinnamon and others. You can definitely choose what to drink with the cake.
